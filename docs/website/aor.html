Areas of Responsibility

Areas of Responsibility serves two purposes. First, it lists those areas that are under active development in the uPortal project and second, it is an expanding list of those tasks/features of uPortal that still need a developer.

If there is a task that you would to contribute work to or become responsible for, click on the Responsible Developers name or "Help Needed" tag and make your request.
