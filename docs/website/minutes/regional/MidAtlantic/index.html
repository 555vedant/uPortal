Mid Atlantic Region uPortal Users Group



The first regional uPortal User's Group Meeting was held January 29, 2002 at Princeton University. Representative from Rutgers, Columbia University, Villanova, and Princeton University attended the meeting.

If you are interested in attending the next MidAtlantic Region uPortal Users Group meeting, please contact, Mark Washington, Rutgers University.

If you are interested in establishing/hosting a uPortal Users Group, contact Carl Jacobson, University of Delaware.
